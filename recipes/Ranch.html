Delicious Ranch Recipe

This is the ultimate Ranch Recipe! I have made several ranch recipes and although some of them are very good, this one is the best!

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 Cup of Whole Buttermilk
  • 1/2 Cup Mayo of your choice
  • 1/2 Cup Sour Cream
  • 2 Buttermilk Ranch Seasoning Packets
  • 1/2 tsp of salt

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients in a bowl
  2. Whisk until mixed well
  3. Sever Cold
